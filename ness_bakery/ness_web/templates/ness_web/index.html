Ness cakes is an establishment that produces and sells flour-based food baked in an oven such as bread, cakes, pastries, and pies.
Flour confectionery includes principally sweet pastries, cakes, and similar baked goods.
Sugar confectionery includes sweets, candied nuts, chocolates, pastillage, and other confections that are made primarily of sugar.
Chocolate confections are treated as a separate category, as are sugar-free versions of sugar confections.
